Smart Legion Lab Team

Smart Legion Lab designs and builds cross‑platform ecosystems — from web and desktop to mobile and CLI. Full‑stack development with 10+ years of expertise — delivering projects from concept to production.

We conduct independent research in fundamental computer science, information theory, and computational complexity. Authors of groundbreaking paradigms including Pointer‑Based Security, Local Data Regeneration, and Position‑Candidate‑Hypothesis (PCH) — with DOI publications and a tech report (Deterministic Game Engine) featuring experimental validation. Our work challenges conventional approaches: eliminating vulnerable data existence, redefining communication as synchronous state discovery, and transforming NP-complete problem-solving through structural-statistical analysis.

Full-cycle delivery: research → architecture → implementation → production. One lab — complete coverage.

Open to consulting, development, research collaborations
