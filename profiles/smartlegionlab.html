About Me

I'm a Software Architect, Full-Stack Developer, and Independent Researcher. I design and build cross-platform applications and ecosystems from the ground up - implementing my own paradigms in working code across web, desktop, mobile, and CLI.

Python is my primary language - 40+ projects, from cryptographic libraries to full-stack Django services. I also work with C#, Kotlin, Go, and JavaScript to deliver consistent logic across platforms.

I am the author of 3 published paradigms in computer security, information theory, and computational complexity - plus a technical report - all with DOI and practical implementations.
