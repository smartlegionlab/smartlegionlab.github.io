About Me

Research Focus

I am a computer scientist and security researcher focused on fundamental paradigm shifts in digital security and information theory. My work challenges conventional approaches by proposing architectural and ontological transformations that redefine how we think about data protection and information transmission.

Technical Expertise

As a Python/Django Architect, I specialize in full-cycle development - from initial architecture to production deployment. I build everything from console applications and libraries to complex web applications and APIs, with a focus on security, scalability, and maintainability.

Development Environment

I work extensively with Linux systems, primarily using Arch Linux for development. This environment provides the flexibility and control needed for advanced security research and system-level programming.

Research Impact

I created the Pointer-Based Security Paradigm and Local Data Regeneration Paradigm, which have gained significant academic attention with proven impact through publication metrics and community engagement.
