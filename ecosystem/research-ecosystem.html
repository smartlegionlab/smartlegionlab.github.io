Research Ecosystem

Research Ecosystem unifies five specialized domains — SmartPassLib Ecosystem for zero-storage password management, Deterministic Ecosystem for infinite deterministic generation and TSP Ecosystem for exact and heuristic route optimization — all built on published research paradigms (Pointer-Based Security, Local Data Regeneration, Position-Candidate-Hypothesis) and implemented across C#, Python, Go, Kotlin, JavaScript, CLI, Desktop, Web, Android, and bash clients.
